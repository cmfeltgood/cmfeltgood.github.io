What is Silly Clock?

Don't you hate how the night and day have differing amounts of daylight, but last the same amount of time? Me too.
Introducing Silly Clock™ :The World's Leading Producer of a Better Time.

Haha, what?

With Silly Clock™ the time of your day and night are adjusted so that 6 a.m. through 6p.m occurs when there is daylight, and 6 p.m. through 6 a.m. occurs when it is dark outside.
